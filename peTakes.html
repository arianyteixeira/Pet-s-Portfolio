About us

by Blair, Lady, Ophelia, Angela & Dieguinho

Every great story has a beginning ours starts with Ophelia, my brother's first cat, who immediately decided she owned the house, the sofa, and everyone in it. Then I walked in with Blair , my first dog in Ireland, a sweet girl who made one thing very clear: she prefers humans, thank you very much. No cats. No drama. Just cuddles with people only.

So my brother, ever the problem solver, brought home Angela to keep Ophelia company because apparently one cat was not enough chaos. Then out of nowhere, Lady showed up and just... stayed. Unexpected, unplanned, and absolutely perfect.

And Dieguinho? He didn't get adopted. He did the adopting. My brother moved into a new house and Dieguinho simply looked around, nodded, and decided he lived there now. No one argued. You don't argue with Dieguinho.

Five pets. Zero chill. Infinite love. ❤️
